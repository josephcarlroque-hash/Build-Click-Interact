About Build, Click, Interact

A web-based simulation platform designed to make UX learning more engaging and accessible through hands-on simulations, interactive challenges, and real-world design scenarios for college students.

UX Principles Applied

Usability

Ensuring the platform is easy to navigate and intuitive for first-time users with clear visual hierarchy and consistent interactions.

Accessibility

Making the platform usable across different devices and screen sizes with responsive design and keyboard navigation support.

User-Centered Design

Building simulations around the actual needs and learning pace of college students with progressive difficulty levels.

Interactivity

Allowing students to actively engage with UX concepts rather than passively consuming information through hands-on exercises.

Target Users

Primary Users

College students enrolled in technology courses seeking practical ways to learn interface design and User Experience Design concepts.

  • Computer Science students
  • Design and UX students
  • Information Technology majors
  • Self-motivated learners

Secondary Users

Instructors who can use it as a supplementary teaching tool and self-learners exploring UX at their own pace.

  • University professors
  • Teaching assistants
  • Workshop facilitators
  • Career changers

Our Learning Approach

Learn by Doing

We believe that the best way to learn User Experience Design is through hands-on experience. Rather than just reading about concepts, students actively engage with interactive simulations that demonstrate real-world design challenges.

Immediate Feedback

Every action in our simulations provides instant feedback, helping students understand why certain design choices work better than others. This immediate reinforcement helps solidify learning concepts.

Progressive Learning

Our modules are structured to build upon each other, starting with fundamental concepts like usability and accessibility, then progressing to more complex topics like user-centered design and interactivity.

Technology Stack

🌐 Frontend

Pure HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (ES6+) - No frameworks required for maximum compatibility and minimal dependencies.

📱 Responsive Design

Mobile-first approach with CSS Grid and Flexbox ensures optimal experience across all devices and screen sizes.

💾 Local Storage

Progress tracking using browser localStorage means students can pick up where they left off without requiring accounts or servers.

🎨 Modern CSS

CSS custom properties, animations, and transitions create smooth, engaging interactions that demonstrate good UX principles.

Expectation vs Reality Quiz

You see blue underlined text on a webpage. What is it?

Users expect blue underlined text to be a link. This is a deeply ingrained mental model.

You see an X button in the top right of a popup.

X in corner = close. This matches users mental model from decades of desktop software.

You see a magnifying glass icon.

Magnifying glass universally represents search in modern interfaces.

You see a hamburger menu icon.

Three horizontal lines now widely represent a hidden navigation menu.

You see a heart icon on a product listing.

Heart icons represent liking or saving items, popularized by social media platforms.

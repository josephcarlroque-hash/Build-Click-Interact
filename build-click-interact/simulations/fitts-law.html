Design Decision Quiz

Where should the most important button be placed?

Center placement is easiest to reach for most users.

A mobile back button is 10px wide. What is wrong?

Mobile touch targets should be at least 44x44px.

Why are Mac menu bars at the top screen edge?

Screen edges stop cursor movement, making targets effectively infinite in one direction.

Which is easier to click?

Larger targets require less precision and are faster to click according to Fitts' Law.
